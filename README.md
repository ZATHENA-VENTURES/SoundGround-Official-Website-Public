## ✨ Key Features

This website is the complete digital home for Sound Ground, featuring:

*   **🏕️ Campsite Booking**: Browse and filter glamping bell tents and primitive campsites with real-time availability and an integrated booking flow.
*   **🏡 Residency Program**: A dedicated page for Sound Ground's long-term live-in program — work-exchange, rentals, and community living on the farm.
*   **🛍️ E-Commerce Shop**: Product listings for merchandise and farm goods with variant selection, cart management, and Stripe checkout.
*   **🌿 Microgreens & Sprouts**: A filterable shop for locally grown microgreens backed by live product data.
*   **🎵 Events & Community**: A community hub listing live music nights, workshops, and gatherings — with ticketing built in.
*   **🎶 Ambient Audio**: Immersive background audio that adapts to day/night themes, with a persistent mute toggle.
*   **🌙 Dynamic Theming**: Day and night modes with a custom animated starry-sky background that persists across sessions.
*   **🖼️ Interactive Gallery**: A filterable image and video gallery with tag filtering, search, and a lightbox viewer.
*   **♿ Accessibility Panel**: Color-blind simulation modes and a hover-to-read dictation mode powered by the Web Speech API.
*   **💬 Testimonials & Map**: Moderated guest reviews, a live review embed, an embedded location map, and a segmented newsletter signup.
*   **📱 Fully Responsive & SEO-Optimized**: A mobile-first design with rich structured data, Open Graph tags, and a full sitemap.
